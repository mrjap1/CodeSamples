Corelli Hosting has been the premier hosting web server company on the Internet since 2001. We provide dedicated servers and reseller hosting services that are able to be matched to our customers' needs across the globe. We currently host over 1,000,000 domains. Corelli Hosting is one of the top 50 hosts in the world, due in part to our custom developed monitoring systems, innovative solutions and continuous data center improvements. Whether you are an individual looking for a basic shared hosting package or your small business needs a scalable dedicated web hosting solution, we are proud to be an industry-leading multi-faceted provider and are willing and able to meet your Web hosting needs.

A fantastic option for many people and businesses is reseller hosting. When you select the Reseller Hosting option, you can choose to use the allotted slots of a shared server for your own sites, or you can resell the open Web hosting slots to 3rd parties who will pay you to host their sites on a shared server. Reseller Hosting is a great way to rent out space that you aren't currently using.

Our dedicated servers support Windows and Linux hosting solutions and come in a variety of sizes and can support any website. While looking through our selection of dedicated Web hosting plans you will notice that we have many different options available. We have basic dedicated servers and dedicated Web hosting plans that are powerful enough to cover even the most demanding needs. We also offer fully managed dedicated servers for any related hosting needs you may have. Furthermore, we offer our clients our state-of-the-art, in-house-developed monitoring service, Synapse, at no extra charge.
